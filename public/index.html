Welcome!

Hi! I'm a private flute instructor based in Houston, Texas, looking for students to teach of all ages and skill levels. Music has been an important part of my life and continues to be so, and I woulld love to share this passion by teaching others the magic it can bring into your life and the lives of those around you.

In High School, I was part of Iowa's All State band for 3 years, and was even selected to be in the All State Orchestra, which only selects 6 flutes in the entire state. In addition to that, I cofounded a Classical Musical Charity Club named Cadenza, which raised over $5,000 during the time I was president and was even featured on news articles like Forbes.

Currently, I am studying Computer Science at Rice Univeristy. However, that has not deterred my pursuit of music. I've been part of Rice's Campanile Orchestra as the principal flautist and have had several performances outside of Orchestra, such as hospitals and campus events. If you're interested in having lessons, feel free to contact me!
